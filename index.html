Services

Weddings

Set up a full sound system for your wedding. This system features Mackie and Behringer powered speakers including an 18” sub­woofer to bring the noise for your dance floor. This package also includes a wireless microphone to use for announcements, MC’ing, and speeches. DJ Klick will make all announcements to ensure the wedding moves smoothly and on schedule. All announcements will be delivered in a simple, professional manner without any cheesy commentary. DJ Klick will communicate with clients and customers months in advance to setup a mix/playlist for all portions of your wedding timeline. The most common portions include: pre­ceremony music, precessional song(s), aisle song(s), recessional song(s), cocktail hour, reception/dinner, garter toss, bouquet toss, cake cutting, first dance, mother/son dance, father/daughter dance, and the dance party. These moments are what makes weddings special and all of them will be delivered with perfect timing and precision. All requested must­play songs will be honored and the do­not­play songs will not even be considered. Every song will have a smooth transition to the next song so you won’t have to worry about any awkward pauses between songs. DJ Klick uses Virtual DJ Pro software paired with a Pioneer DJ controller to ensure the music flows smoothly throughout the wedding. Klick your way to your special day!

Parties

Corporate & Private

Need a DJ for your party? DJ Klick will bring your party to the next level with a professional sound system (12” Mackie speaker, 15” Behringer speaker, and 18” Behringer sub­woofer), Pioneer DJ Controller, and MacBook Pro computer with over 10,000 songs. Every generation dances to a different beat, and DJ Klick will be sure to read your crowd to choose the right music to keep your party popping! A wireless microphone will be included to make announcements/speeches and a light show can really light a fire on your dance floor!

Clubs

Anywhere

DJ Klick has been doing club gigs for the past few years, mainly on Thursdays at the Pastime Club in Truckee, CA. Specializing in Hip­Hop, Pop, and Dance music, DJ Klick starts the night with all the current and classic hip hop hits that we all love, and progressively turns the club into a slammin’ dance party! He is more of hip­hop DJ rather than your full­blown EDM club DJ so keep that in mind when booking for a club gig.
