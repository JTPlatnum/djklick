Weddings

Set up a full sound system for your wedding. This system features Mackie and Behringer powered speakers including an 18” sub­woofer to bring the noise for your dance floor. This package also includes a wireless microphone to use for announcements, MC’ing, and speeches. DJ Klick will handle all announcements if desired. For weddings, DJ Klick will communicate with clients and customers months in advance to setup a mix/playlist for all segments of your wedding timeline.
